Meet the Founder

MS

Manse Soura

Founder & CEO — Vectra Core Systems LLC
U.S. Army Veteran 90% VA Disability AI/ML Engineer AWS Cloud Engineer Oracle SR Engineer

Manse Soura is a combat-decorated U.S. Army veteran and technology executive with deep expertise in enterprise cloud infrastructure, AI/ML systems, and operational logistics. Following military service, he held engineering roles at Oracle and Amazon before advancing to AI/ML Engineer at UMB Financial Corporation.

At UMB, Manse architected and deployed four production-grade conversational AI systems and one autonomous AI agent on Amazon Bedrock, SageMaker, and Amazon Q Business. He holds an MS in Big Data & Information Technology and a BS in Mathematics & Statistics.

Manse founded Vectra Core Systems LLC to bring military precision and cutting-edge technology to the intersection of IT and logistics — serving both government agencies and commercial enterprises that demand reliability, accountability, and results.
