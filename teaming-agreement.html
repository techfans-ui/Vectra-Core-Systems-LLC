6. Confidentiality

Each Party agrees to hold all proprietary and confidential information exchanged in connection with this Agreement in strict confidence. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for three (3) years.

Each Party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property. IP developed jointly under this Agreement shall be addressed in the executed subcontract.
