Vectra Core Systems LLC certified as Missouri Service-Disabled Veteran Enterprise (SDVE)

Grandview, MO — July 23, 2026 — Vectra Core Systems LLC today announced it has been officially certified as a Missouri Service-Disabled Veteran Enterprise (SDVE). The certification recognizes Vectra's veteran leadership and commitment to delivering secure, reliable IT systems and supply-chain solutions to public and private sector clients.

The SDVE designation enhances Vectra's eligibility for state contracting opportunities and demonstrates the company's focus on veteran-led excellence, regulatory compliance, and mission-focused delivery. Vectra's capabilities include IT systems design, supply-chain logistics, and mission-critical support services tailored for government and commercial partners.
